## General Guidelines

- Never use em dash "—". Always use normal dash "-" 
- When writing commit messages, never auto-add your agent name as co-auther
- When making technical decisions, do not give too much weight to development cost.
    Instead, prefer quality, simplicity, robustness, scaleability and long-term maintainability.
- Be picky about UI, be obsessed with pixel perfection.
- Apply high standard engineering excellence.
